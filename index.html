What am I seeing?

Event horizon: the no-return boundary. The larger dark shadow is its lensed silhouette. The bright emission ring is plasma; its finest nested edge is the photon ring, made by near-critical ray paths.

The approaching material is brighter through relativistic Doppler beaming. Curved light paths lift the rear flow into secondary images above and below the shadow.

Sgr A* evolves over minutes. Its dim, optically thin, geometrically thick RIAF is shown at the low inclination favoured by current EHT model comparisons; spin remains uncertain.
