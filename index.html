What am I seeing?

Event horizon: the no-return boundary. The larger dark shadow is its lensed silhouette. The bright emission ring is hot plasma; its finest nested edge is the photon ring, made by near-critical ray paths.

The approaching plasma is brighter through relativistic Doppler beaming. Curved light paths lift the rear of the thick flow into secondary images above and below the shadow.

Sgr A* is compact enough to evolve over minutes: the flare model defaults to a ~70-minute orbit near the ISCO. This is a physically motivated, Kerr-inspired radiative model, not a research-grade GRMHD calculation.
